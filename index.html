The Little Swan that Goosed!

A Website by Larswan the Goose

Once upon a time there was a little swan who felt so very strange... For as long as it could remember the swan had wanted to be a goose! Each fall, wading in the creek with the rest of its family the swan would gander in awe as a distant V pierced through the sky. It heard the trumpets of a hundred geese honk by on their yearly treck southward to greener pastures.

Today you can make this little swan's dreams come true! Wave your magic mouse over it to transform it into a beautiful goose!
